Scoutly: Job Tracker

Capture job applications from LinkedIn, Workday, Lever, and Oracle Taleo. Sync them to a Google Sheet you control. No server. Your data stays in your account.

Add to Chrome

What it does

  • One click Google Sheets connect
  • Auto capture role, company, location, date, and source link
  • Duplicate prevention
  • Status tracking in your sheet: Applied, Interviewing, Accepted, Rejected, Withdrawn

How it works

  1. Install from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Open the popup and choose Connect Google
  3. Select or create a Google Sheet
  4. Click Log to add postings as you browse

Data and permissions

The extension requests the Google Sheets scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets only to insert and update rows in the sheet you select. It stores the spreadsheet ID and basic settings in your browser. No backend. No sharing or selling of data.
